## LinkedIn Public Post

I just shipped my first UI/UX case study: Osteóplus, a PWA-first clinic product for adults 60+ in Barcelona.

The decision I am most proud of: shifting the product from a medical repository model to an action dashboard.

The repository asked a senior patient to browse, remember, interpret, and decide. The action dashboard surfaces the next meaningful action: book, attend, start today's exercise.

For a patient who forgets exercises between visits, that ordering matters more than any feature inventory.

Inside the case study:

- Strategic IA shift, with rejected alternatives and trade-offs
- WCAG 2.2 AA at the token level: 48/56px targets, focus rings, reduced motion
- Clinically safe gamification: no punitive streak loss, pain interrupt modal
- Bilingual ES-first / EN-secondary product thinking
- Evidence discipline: documented vs. expected vs. recommended labels throughout

It is a concept-stage project, transparent about validation gaps. First portfolio, but it is the work I want to be judged on.

Feedback welcome from anyone in health tech, accessibility, or senior-friendly design.

Case study: https://case-study-osteoplus.vercel.app/

Optional hashtags: `#UX` `#Accessibility` `#Healthcare`

## Outreach Rules

- Start with 5-10 personalized messages, not a large batch.
- Prioritize health tech, accessibility, rehab, wellness, senior-care, and product teams.
- Personalize at least one sentence before sending.
- Schedule the first follow-up 5-7 days after the initial message.
- Send no more than two follow-ups. After 14 days with no response, move on.
- Do not imply the product has shipped. Keep the concept-stage framing honest.
- If a repeated critique appears, improve the case study before sending the next batch.
