## 4. Annotation conventions

Annotation type, color, lines, callouts, patterns, and per-asset application are documented in full in `07-figma-annotation-conventions.md`. Read that file before producing any annotated asset (VIS-01–06, SOL-05, SOL-07, A11Y-02, IA-01, PROC-02, PROB-01).

Quick reference:

- **Boundary lines.** 1px dotted, `#94A3B8` (slate-400), 4–2 dash pattern.
- **Leader lines.** 1.5px solid, `#64748B` (slate-500), no arrowhead, 4px dot at target end.
- **Labels.** 14px sans, `#334155` (slate-700), with 4px white outline at 80% opacity for legibility over UI.
- **Contrast pills.** Pill, slate-100 fill, mono 12px, format `15.8 : 1` with thin space around the colon. Pass badge ≥ 4.5 : 1, fail otherwise.
- **Token annotations.** Format `color/brand/primary · #0F766E`, monospace 13px.
- **Hot spots.** 12px brand teal-700 fill with 4px teal-500 30% ring, numbered when 2+.
- **Focus rings.** 2px real teal-500 (`#14B8A6`), demonstrate on one element only per screen.
- **No arrowheads on leader lines.** Use a 4px dot at the target end.
- **No brand colors in annotation chrome** except hot spots and focus rings.

## 5. Quality bar (per asset)

Before shipping any asset:

- [ ] Exported at 2× pixel density for retina (PNG/WebP).
- [ ] WebP fallback or AVIF for performance.
- [ ] `alt` text written (in `_data/` content array, not in filename).
- [ ] Dark theme variant produced if the section shows light/dark.
- [ ] No real patient data, no real practitioner names, no Carmen as a real person.
- [ ] No logos or imagery from competing clinics or third parties.
- [ ] All copy on the mockup itself is the canonical Spanish-first copy from `Deck_Complete_ES_EN.md`.
