## 6. Non-negotiable principles

- WCAG 2.2 Level AA accessibility
- 48px minimum touch targets; 56px for high-priority patient actions
- Plain-language copy over medical jargon
- Spanish-first, English-secondary localization
- PWA-first delivery
- Guest-first booking
- Clinically safe gamification — adherence without pressure
- Evidence-based claims only
